This is Your Role Playing Scenario

You are a teacher in the faculty of Engineering, and have been asked to teach a face to face coding course to Bachelor students. Your class has 43 students. In the second session of your class you will need to create a collaborative learning activity for checking if your students understood what was explained in class e.g by asking them to define the key theoretical concepts presented in that class (Remember task based on Bloom's taxonomy). You are estimating this collaborative activity will take 45 minutes. PyramidApp is the platform that you heard about. You may want to explore the current LDs in the PyramidApp and you may get surprised by knowing that there is a system that helps you to do so by providing recommendations on learning designs.
